The Story of the Spoon

Where tradition meets innovation.

The Pursuit of Perfection

The Gilded Spoon was born from a simple yet profound idea: to create exquisite timepieces that honor the rich heritage of horology while embracing the innovations of tomorrow. We believe a watch is more than an instrument for telling time; it is a wearable piece of art, a personal statement, and a legacy to be passed down through generations.

Our journey began in the heart of Paris, where our founders spent years mastering the intricate craft of French cuisine. Every ingredient, every technique, and every presentation at The Gilded Spoon is a result of that lifelong dedication.
